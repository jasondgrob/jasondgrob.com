Homelab & Labs

A place to break things safely – and learn from it

My homelab isn’t a toy cluster; it’s a structured environment for practicing what I preach: segmented networks, layered controls, and observable systems that behave like production.

Core lab design
Modeled after real enterprises
  • Multiple VLANs for management, trusted devices, workstations, IoT, servers, test, and VPN.
  • Active Directory domain with structured OUs, group-based policy, and proper DNS integration.
  • Pi-hole + Unbound design where AD DNS, recursive resolution, and filtering all play their own roles.
Zero-trust-friendly layout DNS as a first-class citizen
